Before you delete

Consider exporting any info you need (screenshots of results, notes, or achievements).

Need a pause?

If you just want to stop notifications, change settings instead of deleting.

What will be deleted

Data removal

This is what gets removed from your account when deletion is confirmed:

Account information

username, phone number, device ID, and profile data.

Learning progress

Study history, exam scores, and performance metrics.

Achievements

Badges, milestones, and completion records.

App settings

Preferences, notification settings, and customizations.

Support history

Support requests and past communications (if any).

Usage data

Sessions, feature usage, and analytics linked to your account.

How to delete your account

Step-by-step

Click each step to expand details.

1 Open the app
Launch the Drive Rwanda – Prep & Pass app and log in to your account.
2 Go to Settings
Open your profile/settings area, then choose “Account Settings” or “Profile Settings”.
3 Find “Delete Account”
Scroll to the bottom of settings to find “Delete Account” / “Account Deletion”.
4 Confirm your identity
Enter your password or confirm identity as requested to continue.
5 Read the warning carefully
Make sure you understand what data will be deleted and that the action is irreversible.
6 Type confirmation text
Type DELETE in the confirmation field to acknowledge the consequences.
7 Final confirmation
Tick the checkbox (if shown) and press the “Delete Account” button to complete deletion.
8 Account deleted
You will be logged out and redirected to the login screen. Data removal is permanent.
No data recovery

After deletion, we cannot restore learning progress, scores, profile details, or settings.
