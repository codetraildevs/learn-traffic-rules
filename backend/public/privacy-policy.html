Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data Protection

Overview

Educational Purpose

Learn Traffic Rules is an educational application designed to help users prepare for provisional driving license examinations. This app is not affiliated with any government agency and serves as a practice tool only.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our educational traffic rules learning application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password
  • Learning Progress: Exam scores, study history, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, session duration, and feature interactions
  • Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, and customizations

How We Use Information

Purpose of Data Collection

  • Educational Services: Provide personalized learning experiences and track progress
  • Performance Analytics: Analyze learning patterns to improve educational content
  • Notifications: Send study reminders and educational updates
  • App Improvement: Enhance app functionality and user experience
  • Security: Protect against fraud and ensure account security

Data Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

Your Rights

Data Protection Rights

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request data export in a portable format
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing
