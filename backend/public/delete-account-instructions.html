Delete Account

Learn how to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Permanent Action

Important Information

⚠️ This Action Cannot Be Undone

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data from our servers. This includes your profile, learning progress, exam results, and all other information associated with your account.

Educational Purpose Notice

Learn Traffic Rules is an educational application designed to help users prepare for provisional driving license examinations. This app is not affiliated with any government agency and serves as a practice tool only.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account Information: Email, username, password, and profile data
  • Learning Progress: All exam scores, study history, and performance metrics
  • Achievements: All badges, certificates, and learning milestones
  • App Settings: Preferences, notification settings, and customizations
  • Support History: All previous support tickets and communications
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, session data, and analytics

How to Delete Your Account

1
Open the App

Launch the Learn Traffic Rules app on your device and log in to your account.

2
Navigate to Settings

Go to your profile or settings section within the app. Look for "Account Settings" or "Profile Settings".

3
Find Delete Account Option

Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" or "Account Deletion" option. This is usually located at the bottom of the settings page.

4
Confirm Your Identity

You may be asked to enter your password or confirm your identity to proceed with the deletion.

5
Read the Warning

Carefully read the warning message that explains what will be deleted and that this action cannot be undone.

6
Type Confirmation Text

Type "DELETE" in the confirmation field to acknowledge that you understand the consequences.

7
Final Confirmation

Check the confirmation checkbox and click the "Delete Account" button to permanently delete your account.

8
Account Deleted

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. You will be logged out and redirected to the login screen.

Alternative Methods

Contact Support

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, you can contact our support team directly. We'll help you delete your account manually.

Email Request

Send an email to codetrail.dev@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your account details.

Data Recovery

No Data Recovery

Important: Once your account is deleted, we cannot recover any of your data. This includes:

  • Learning progress and exam scores
  • Personal information and profile data
  • App settings and preferences
  • Support history and communications
