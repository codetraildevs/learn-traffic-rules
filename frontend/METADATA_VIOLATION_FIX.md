## ‚ö†Ô∏è What to Avoid Going Forward

### ‚ùå NEVER USE:
- **Performance claims**: "Best", "#1", "Top", "Thousands", "Millions"
- **Testimonials**: "Users say...", "Join thousands...", "Success stories"
- **Rankings**: "Top rated", "Award winning", "Most popular"
- **Success rates**: "95% pass rate", "X users passed"
- **Promotional words**: "Free", "Discount", "Sale" (unless genuinely applicable)

### ‚úÖ ALWAYS USE:
- **Feature descriptions**: "Interactive practice tests with detailed explanations"
- **Educational value**: "Designed for provisional driving license preparation"
- **Aspirational language**: "Start your journey...", "Get ready for..."
- **Functional benefits**: "Track progress", "Study offline", "Practice anytime"
- **Clear disclaimers**: "Educational practice only", "Not affiliated with government"
